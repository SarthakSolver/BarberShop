About Us

we are working for minimizing wait time for customer for service providing shop ex.Barber Shop

In today's fast-paced world, service-providing shops such as barber shops, salons, clinics, and repair centers often face the challenge of managing long queues and reducing customer wait times. Traditional queue management methods, such as manual entries and "first-come, firstserve" systems, can lead to inefficiency, overcrowding, and customer dissatisfaction. In order to enhance customer experience and improve operational efficiency, there is a growing need for smart queue management systems.
